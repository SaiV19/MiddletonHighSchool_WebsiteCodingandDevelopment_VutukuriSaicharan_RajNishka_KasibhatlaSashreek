About the role


The Financial Manager will play a crucial role in managing and optimizing the financial health of our organization's activities. This individual will be responsible for financial planning, analysis, budgeting, and reporting, ensuring the efficient and sustainable financial management of our initiatives.



What you would do


  • Develop and maintain financial models to support strategic decision-making for VitaFloraprojects
  • Conduct financial analysis and provide insights to drive cost-effective and efficient operations
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment of financial goals with overall organizational objectives
  • Prepare annual budgets for VitaFlora operations, considering resource requirements, revenue projections, and cost management
  • Monitor budget performance regularly and provide variance analysis
  • Implement cost control measures to ensure efficient use of resources in VitaFlora projects
  • Work closely with project managers to monitor and manage project-related expenses
  • Prepare and present financial reports to senior management, highlighting key performance indicators and financial metrics related to VitaFlora initiatives
  • Identify and assess financial risks associated with VitaFlora operations
  • Develop and implement risk mitigation strategies to safeguard the financial interests of the organization


What We Expect


  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field (Master's degree preferred)
  • Proven experience as a Financial Manager or similar role, preferably in a botanical or environmental organization
  • Strong financial modeling and analysis skills
  • Excellent understanding of budgeting, forecasting, and financial reporting
  • Knowledge of relevant regulatory requirements and accounting standards
  • Effective communication and collaboration skills
