Insurances


Having good insurance is essential with today's world and has become a common staple, I mean have you seen anyone lately without proper insurance? But finding quality insurance for cheap these days, practically impossible. It's unheard of really. With all that in mind we offer our employees all kinds of insurances. Health, dental, life, it's all covered! Our insurance agents will set you up with top-level insurance companies in your area, only the best for our employees. So next time you're in an accident or need to visit the doctor, VitaFlora has your covered.







Retirement Planning

We want to make sure that when you'll unfortunately be leaving us, we aren't the only ones tearing up right, you'll be taken care of. You should enjoy your retirement and relax, not have to constantly worry about bills and finding funds. In order to prevent all that we provide all our employees with retirement plans from their first day of working with us. Our retirement plans include 401(k) plans, employee stock ownership plans, and 403(b) plan. Now this may seem like a big decision to make, after all retirement is no small thing. But don't worry our financial consultants will work with you around the clock to make sure that whatever plan you choose, you'll be set for life.



Paid Time Off(PTO)


As much as a priority the company is, your personal life is an even bigger priority. That's why we offer PTO to all our employees. Sometimes all we need to do to refresh our minds and our bodies is to just take some time off and relax that way we can come back to work better than ever. So whether you have to take parental time off to attend your child, need some rest when you're sick and not feeling well, or to just go on vacation and have some fun, we'll make sure your able to.







Tuition Reimbursement

We can all agree that the worst part about pursuing a higher education is definitely the endless student loans. They're just such a burden to deal with it. That's why VitaFlora helps you pay of those horrendous loans. We offer different types of plans to help meet your requirements, everyone's accounted for! No catch, no twist, no nothing. So loans that would've taken maybe 9-10 years to pay off, can be fully paid off in just 3-4 years!



Career Development Opportunities


We know the potential of our employees, they're destined to achieve greatness. We simply want to help you achieve that. Here you get trained and taught by the best in the field ensuring that you'll be a pro in no time. So with our career development workshops, training programs, mentorship programs, you'll be able to reach that greatness before you even know it.







Flexibile Working

VitaFlora provides you with a flexible environment in order for you to meet all your personal needs. But it's not just time that's flexible here, at VitaFlora you won't have to constantly be stuck in the office as you can also work from home when needed.



Employee Awards and Recognition


We appreciate all the work, time, and dedication that all of our outstanding employees put in to our company each and every day. We know it's hard waking up in the morning to come to work, don't we all just want to sleep in! But without our employees our company just could not exist, they're the foundation of VitaFlora. We believe good deeds should always be rewarded! That's why we offer numerous awards and recognition programs to acknowledge and recognize those efforts and contributions.







Traveling

Who doesn't like to travel. The adventure, the thrill, the fun, what's there not to like. Many of our positions get numerous opportunities to travel to various countries and cities. Explore the culture, try new food, and discover a whole new lifestyle, the possibilities are endless. So you'll be able to explore the world all while working your dream job.



Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs)


At VitaFlora we have consultants constantly working to help you with your personal life. Here we're a family, and family take care of each other at all times. So whether it's to help your fight those legal battles or take control of your financial life, are experts will make sure that you come out on top.







Product Discounts For All Employees

It's only fair that our employees get discounts on the products they spend so much time working on. That's why as an employee you'll receive a range of discounts on all of VitaFlora's products!
