Who Are We?

VitaFlora is a company where we make an alternative to everyday medical products, a herbal alternative. We're not just a company looking to make a profit, we're passionate advocates for a natural life. We source our ingredients straight to the root of all medicine, plants! That's right our products are completely made from plants and other flora species. Our team gather the flora specimens and study them finding the purposes and benefits of the plant, to transform them into products. We make our products with the genuine care and make sure each product is the best it can be. Our products are shown in our own companies fashion and are uniquely made to help address problem. We are a community on a journey to wellness and a healthier lives for all! VitaFlora offers many positions as the field of medicine is always changing and we are always looking to expand and make even more healthier, plant based alternatives.






Our Vision


Here at VitaFlora we strive to put forward an alternative way of regular medicine. Our goal is to provide herbal, plant-based medicine for all. We've personally seen how damaging manufactured medicine can be to people. We're simply offering you a way to heal yourself without other negatively impacting side effects.







Diversity and Equality

Here at VitaFlora we have a ZERO discrimination tolerance policy and provide complete equality for all. We believe everyone deserves the same chances and opportunities. So when you're working with us you'll never have to worry about any discrimination. Aside of having an equal work environment, we also have a nontoxic work environment. We've all seen the horrible bosses and managers mis-treating their employees. We put our employees well-being at the utmost importance. We make sure everyone is treated with the kindness and respect they deserve.
